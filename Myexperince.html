üëúInternship Experience

During my internship in JLMWT, I had the incredible opportunity to visit the One Fathom Bank Lighthouse, Malaysia's only lighthouse located at sea. The iconic building is in the Malacca Strait, an important and busy nautical route. The lighthouse itself is amazing in size, with its light beam visible for up to 42 nautical miles (approximately 30 kilometers from Klang). The location is somewhat exclusive, and not everyone can access the lighthouse, making this experience truly unique and unforgettable for me.
